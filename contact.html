How to Join DD Coder Dojo

1

Contact Us

Fill out the form above or call us at 031 461 3101 to express your interest and ask any questions.

2

Collect Forms

Visit our office at 178 Austerville Drive to collect the indemnity form and registration documents.

3

Complete Registration

Fill in, sign, and submit the completed forms along with any required documentation.

4

Attend Orientation

Join our orientation session to learn about our programs, meet mentors, and get started coding!

5

Join Community

Scan our QR code to join the WhatsApp community for updates, announcements, and support.
